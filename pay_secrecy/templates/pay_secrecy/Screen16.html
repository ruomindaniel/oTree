In each period, employee's earnings are:

  • Employee’s initial balance (20 points)
  • Employee’s effort (0 to 20 points)
  • Employee’s individual bonus (15% of individual output)
  • Employee’s share of the bonus pool (allocated by the manager)
  • Employee’s earnings for the period

In each period, manager's earnings are:

  • Manager’s initial balance (10 points)
  • Manager’s bonus (a portion of total firm output)
  • Manager’s earnings for the period
