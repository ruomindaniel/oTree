This task is called the slider bar task. On the next screen, you will see a large number of sliders that are initially positioned at 0 along a scale from 0 to 100. Your goal is to position each slider at exactly 50.

The slider can be adjusted and readjusted an unlimited number of times. Everyone has the same slider task. The computer will count the number of sliders you correctly positioned.

Please spend some time to familiarize yourself with the task. Click “Start Practice” to start the practice period and the practice period will automatically end after 60 seconds.
